2\mathrm{SM} - \mathrm{one - half}\) statute mile

(a) Tower/surface visibility. If either visibility (tower or surface) is below four statute miles,

the lesser of the two will be reported in the body of the report; the greater will be reported in remarks.

(b) Automated visibility. ASOS/AWSS visibility stations will show visibility 10 or greater than 10 miles as "10SM." AWOS visibility stations will show visibility less than \(1 / 4\) statute mile as "M \(1 / 4\) SM" and visibility 10 or greater than 10 miles as "10SM."
