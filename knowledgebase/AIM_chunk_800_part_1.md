## NOTE-

AC 90- 100A does not apply to over water RNAV routes (reference 14 CFR 91.511, including the Q-routes in the Gulf of Mexico and the Atlantic routes) or Alaska VOR/DME RNAV routes ("JxxR"). The AC does not apply to off- route RNAV operations, Alaska GPS routes or Caribbean routes.

(1) Q-routes are available for use by RNAV equipped aircraft between 18,000 feet MSL and FL 450 inclusive. Q-routes are depicted on Enroute High Altitude Charts.
