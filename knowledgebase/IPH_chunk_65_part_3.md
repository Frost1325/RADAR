Pilots departing an airport under IFR and operating under Part 121 or 135 are required by 14 CFR 91.175(f)(4) to use an engine- inoperative takeoff obstacle clearance or avoidance procedure that assures compliance with the obstacle clearance requirements (subpart I) of those rules. The assessment of OEl takeoff obstacle clearance is separate and independent of the IFR departure procedure and associated all- engines- operating climb gradient requirements. While the Part 91 operating rules governing large, commuter, and turbine- powered aircraft do not require the use of an OEl takeoff obstacle clearance or avoidance procedure, such use is encouraged for Part 91 operators of these aircraft.
