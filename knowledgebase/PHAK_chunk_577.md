## Speed Ranges

The speed of sound varies with temperature. Under standard temperature conditions of \(15^{\circ}\mathrm{C}\) , the speed of sound at sea level is 661 knots. At 40,000 feet, where the temperature is \(- 55^{\circ}\mathrm{C}\) , the speed of sound decreases to 574 knots. In high

speed flight and/or high- altitude flight, the measurement of speed is expressed in terms of a "Mach number"- the ratio of the true airspeed of the aircraft to the speed of sound in the same atmospheric conditions.
