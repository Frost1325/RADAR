SIGMETs, international SIGMETs are also identified by an alphabetic designator from Alpha through Mike and are numbered sequentially until that weather phenomenon ends. The criteria for an international SIGMET are:

(a) Thunderstorms occurring in lines, embedded in clouds, or in large areas producing tornadoes or large hail.

(b) Tropical cyclones.
(c) Severe icing.
(d) Severe or extreme turbulence.
(e) Dust storms and sandstorms lowering visibilities to less than 3 miles.
(f) Volcanic ash.
