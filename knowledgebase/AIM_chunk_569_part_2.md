c. RVSM Authorization. In accordance with 14 CFR Section 91.180, with only limited exceptions, prior to operating in RVSM airspace, operators and aircraft must have received RVSM authorization from the responsible civil aviation authority. (See Paragraph 4-6-10, Procedures for Accommodation of Non-RVSM Aircraft.)
