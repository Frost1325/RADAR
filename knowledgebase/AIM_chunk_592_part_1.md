## 4-7-2. Gulf of Mexico 50 NM Lateral Separation Initiative Web Page: Policy, Procedures and Guidance for Operators and Regulators

Information on plans, policies and procedures for 50 NM lateral separation is posted on the "Gulf of Mexico 50 NM Lateral Separation Web Page":

http://www.faa.gov/about/office_org/headquarters_offices/ato/service_units/enroute/oceanic/comex/

The web page contains detailed guidance on operator and aircraft authorization for RNP 10 or RNP 4 and includes Job Aids with FAA and ICAO document references.
