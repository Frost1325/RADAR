Up to four lines of minima are included on these charts. Ground Based Augmentation System (GBAS) Landing System (GLS) was a placeholder for future WAAS and LAAS minima, and the minima was always listed as N/A. The GLS minima line has now been replaced by the WAAS LPV (Localizer Performance with Vertical Guidance) minima on most RNAV (GPS) charts. LNAV/VNAV (lateral navigation/vertical navigation) was added to support both WAAS electronic vertical guidance and Barometric VNAV.

LPV and LNAV/VNAV are both APV procedures as described in paragraph 5- 4- 5a7. The original GPS minima, titled "S- XX," for straight in runway XX, is retitled LNAV (lateral navigation).
