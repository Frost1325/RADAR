## PHRASEOLOGY-

"(Aircraft call sign) YOU HAVE CROSSED THE FINAL APPROACH COURSE. TURN (left/right) IMMEDIATELY AND RETURN TO THE LOCALIZER FINAL APPROACH COURSE,"

or "(aircraft call sign) TURN (left/right) AND RETURN TO THE LOCALIZER FINAL APPROACH COURSE."

(f) If a deviating aircraft fails to respond to such instructions or is observed penetrating the NTZ, the aircraft on the adjacent final approach course (if threatened) will be issued a breakout instruction.
