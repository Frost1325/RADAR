With an electrical system failure and associated loss of an attitude indication, a pilot may find the aircraft in an unusual attitude. In this situation, established procedures are used to regain SA and aircraft control. Pilots must be alert to loss of SA especially when hampered by a reactive mindset. To regain SA, reassess the situation and work toward understanding what the problem is. The pilot may need to seek additional information from other sources, such as navigation instruments, other crewmembers, or ATC.
