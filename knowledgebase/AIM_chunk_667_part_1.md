## NOTE-

ICAO location indicators must consist of 4 letters. Airport identifiers such as 51A7, 39LL and Z40 are not in ICAO standard format.

(b) If no four-letter location indicator has been assigned to the departure aerodrome, insert ZZZZ and specify the non-ICAO location identifier, or fix/radial/distance from a nearby naval, followed by the name of the aerodrome, in Item 18, following characters DEP/.

(c) Then, without a space, insert the estimated off-block time.

EXAMPLE- 1. KSMF2215

2. ZZZZ0330
