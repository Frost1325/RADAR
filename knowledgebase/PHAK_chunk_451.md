Manufacturers try to reach a midpoint between too much and too little directional stability. Because it is more desirable for the aircraft to have "spiral instability" than Dutch roll tendencies, most aircraft are designed with that characteristic.

## Spiral Instability

Spiral instability exists when the static directional stability of the aircraft is very strong as compared to the effect of its dihedral in maintaining lateral equilibrium.
