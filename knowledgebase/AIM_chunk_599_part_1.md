## 4-7-8. Flight Planning Requirements

Operators must make ICAO flight plan annotations in accordance with this paragraph and, if applicable, Paragraph 4- 7- 5, Provisions for Accommodation of NonRNP10 Aircraft (Aircraft Not Authorized RNP 10 or RNP 4).

a. ICAO Flight Plan Requirement. ICAO flight plans must be filed for operation on oceanic routes and areas in the Houston Oceanic CTA/FIR, the Gulf of Mexico portion of the Miami CTA/FIR, the Monterrey CTA and Merida High CTA.

b. To inform ATC that they have obtained RNP 10 or RNP 4 authorization and are eligible for \(50~\mathrm{NM}\) lateral separation, operators must:

1. Annotate ICAO Flight Plan Item 10 (Equipment) with the letter "R" and
2. Annotate Item 18 (Other Information) with, as appropriate, "PBN/A1" (for RNP10) or "PBN/L1" (for RNP4).
