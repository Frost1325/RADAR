## Converting Minutes to Equivalent Hours

Converting Minutes to Equivalent HoursFrequently, it is necessary to convert minutes into equivalent hours when solving speed, time, and distance problems. To convert minutes to hours, divide by \(60 (60 \text{ minutes} = 1 \text{ hour})\) . Thus, 30 minutes is \(30 / 60 = 0.5 \text{ hour}\) . To convert hours to minutes, multiply by 60. Thus, 0.75 hour equals \(0.75 \times 60 = 45 \text{ minutes}\) .
