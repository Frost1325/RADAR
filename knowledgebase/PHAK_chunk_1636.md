## VFR Terminal Area Charts

VFR Terminal Area ChartsVFR terminal area charts are helpful when flying in or near Class B airspace. They have a scale of \(1:250,000\) (1 inch \(= 3.43\) NM or approximately 4 SM). These charts provide a more detailed display of topographical information and are revised semiannually, except for several Alaskan and Caribbean charts. [Figure 16- 2]
