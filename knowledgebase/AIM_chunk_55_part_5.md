(7) Overriding an automatically selected sensitivity during an approach will cancel the approach mode annunciation. If the approach mode is not armed by \(2\mathrm{NM}\) prior to the FAWP, the approach mode will not become active at \(2\mathrm{NM}\) prior to the FAWP, and the equipment will flag.
