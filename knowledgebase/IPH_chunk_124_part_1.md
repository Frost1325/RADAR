## Dual VOR Check

If a VOT or VOR checkpoint is not available and the aircraft is equipped with dual VORs, the equipment may be checked against one another by tuning both sets to the VOR facility at the same time and noting the indicated bearings to that station. [Figure 2- 52] A difference greater than \(4^{\circ}\) between

the two VORs indicates that one of the receivers may be out of tolerance.
