 the maximum speed at which the limit load can be imposed (either by gusts or full deflection of the control surfaces) without causing structural damage. Operating at or below maneuvering speed does not provide structural protection against multiple full control inputs in one axis or full control inputs in more than one axis at the same time.

\(\mathrm{V_{NO}}\) the maximum speed for normal operation or the maximum structural cruising speed. This is the speed at which exceeding the limit load factor may cause permanent deformation of the aircraft structure.

\(\mathrm{V_{NE}}\) the speed that should never be exceeded. If flight is attempted above this speed, structural damage or structural failure may result.
