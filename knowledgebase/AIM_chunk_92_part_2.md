(2) RNP AR APCH. RNP AR APCH procedures are titled RNAV (RNP). RNP AR APCH vertical navigation performance is based upon barometric VNAV or WAAS. RNP AR is intended to provide specific benefits at specific locations. It is not intended for every operator or aircraft. RNP AR capability requires specific aircraft performance, design, operational processes, training, and specific procedure design criteria to achieve the required target level of safety.
