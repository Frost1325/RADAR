f. "Cleared to (destination) airport as filed" does NOT include the en route altitude filed in a flight plan.

An en route altitude will be stated in the clearance or the pilot will be advised to expect an assigned or filed altitude within a given time frame or at a certain point after departure. This may be done verbally in the departure instructions or stated in the DP.
