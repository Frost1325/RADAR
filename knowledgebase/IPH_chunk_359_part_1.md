For an operator to proceed VFR under Part 135, a minimum visibility of \(\frac{1}{2}\) SM during the day and 1 SM at night with a minimum ceiling of 300 feet. If prior to commencing the approach, the pilot determines the reported visibility is \(\frac{1}{4}\) SM during the day, the pilot descends IMC to an altitude no lower than the MDA and transitions to VMC.
