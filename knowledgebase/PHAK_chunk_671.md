The jet- fueled piston engine's technology has continued to progress and a full authority digital engine control (FADEC, discussed more fully later in the chapter) is standard on such equipped aircraft, which minimizes complication of engine control. By 2007, various jet- fueled piston aircraft had logged well over 600,000 hours of service.
