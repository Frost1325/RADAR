The radius of turn (R) can be computed using a simple formula. The radius of turn is equal to the velocity squared \((V^2)\) divided by 11.26 times the tangent of the bank angle.

\[\mathrm{R} = \frac{\mathrm{V}^2}{11.26\times \mathrm{tangent~of~bank~angle}}\]
