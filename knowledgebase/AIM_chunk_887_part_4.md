A stuck microphone may block the issuance of ATC instructions on the tower frequency by the final monitor controller during simultaneous (parallel) independent and simultaneous close parallel PRM approaches. In the case of PRM approaches, the use of a second frequency by the monitor controller mitigates the "stuck mike" or other blockage on the tower frequency.
