V

\(\mathbf{V}_{1}\) . Critical engine failure speed or takeoff decision speed. It is the speed at which the pilot is to continue the takeoff in the event of an engine failure or other serious emergency. At speeds less than \(\mathrm{V}_{1}\) , it is considered safer to stop the aircraft within the accelerate- stop distance. It is also the minimum speed in the takeoff, following a failure of the critical engine at \(\mathrm{V}_{\mathrm{EF}}\) , at which the pilot can continue the takeoff and achieve the required height above the takeoff surface within the takeoff distance.
