VERY HIGH FREQUENCY- The frequency band between 30 and \(300\mathrm{MHz}\) . Portions of this band, 108 to \(118\mathrm{MHz}\) , are used for certain NAVAIDs; 118 to \(136\mathrm{MHz}\) are used for civil air/ground voice communications. Other frequencies in this band are used for purposes not related to air traffic control.
