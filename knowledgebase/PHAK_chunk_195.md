Transfer—Should this risk decision be transferred to someone else (e.g., do you need to consult the chief flight instructor?) Eliminate—Is there a way to eliminate the hazard? Accept—Do the benefits of accepting risk outweigh the costs? Mitigate—What can you do to mitigate the risk?

The goal is to perform by taking action to eliminate hazards or mitigate risk, and then continuously evaluate the outcome of this action.
