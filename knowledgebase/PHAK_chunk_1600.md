## Local Airport Advisory (LAA)

Local Airport Advisory (LAA)An advisory service provided by Flight Service Station (FSS) facilities, which are located on the landing airport, using a discrete ground- to- air frequency or the tower frequency when the tower is closed. LAA services include local airport advisories, automated weather reporting with voice broadcasting, and a continuous Automated Surface Observing System (ASOS)/Automated Weather Observing Station (AWOS) data display, other continuous direct reading instruments, or manual observations available to the specialist.
