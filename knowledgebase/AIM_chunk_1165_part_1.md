## 3. Terminal Doppler Weather Radar (TDWR).

(a) TDWRs are being deployed at 45 locations across the U.S. Optimum locations for TDWRs are 8 to 12 miles off of the airport proper, and designed to look at the airspace around and over the airport to detect microbursts, gust fronts, wind shifts

and precipitation intensities. TDWR products advise the controller of wind shear and microburst events impacting all runways and the areas \(1 / 2\) mile on either side of the extended centerline of the runways out to 3 miles on final approach and 2 miles out on departure.
