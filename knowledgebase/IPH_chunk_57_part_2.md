The use of the aforementioned visibility reports and weather services are not limited for Part 91 operators. Part 121 and 135 operators are bound by their individual OpSpecs documents and are required to use weather reports that come from the NWS or other approved sources. While every operator's specifications are individually tailored, most operators are required to use ATIS, RVR reports, and selected reports from automated weather stations.
