c. Strategic Lateral Offset Procedures (SLOP). Pilots should use SLOP procedures in the course of regular oceanic operations. SLOP procedures are published in ICAO Document 4444, 15th Edition, Amendment 2, paragraph 16.5 and FAA Notices. They are posted on the Gulf of Mexico 50 NM Lateral Separation Web Page and are addressed in the RNP 10 and RNP 4 Job Aids.
