The 48 conterminous states of the United States are between \(67^{\circ}\) and \(125^{\circ}\) W longitude. The arrows in Figure 16- 4 labeled "Longitude" point to lines of longitude.

Any specific geographical point can be located by reference to its longitude and latitude. Washington, D.C., for example, is approximately \(39^{\circ}\) N latitude, \(77^{\circ}\) W longitude. Chicago is approximately \(42^{\circ}\) N latitude, \(88^{\circ}\) W longitude.
