## Common Errors

Common errors with forward slips to a landing:

Common errors with forward slips to a landing:1. Incorrect pitch adjustments that result in poor airspeed control.2. Reacting to erroneous airspeed indications.3. Using excess power while trying to lose altitude.4. A slip in the same direction as any crosswind.5. Poor glidepath control.6. Late transition to a sideslip during landing with crosswinds.7. Landing without the longitudinal axis parallel to runway.8. Landing off the centerline.
