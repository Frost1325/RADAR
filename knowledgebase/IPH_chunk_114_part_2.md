996R. [Figure 2- 45] The R suffix is a supplementary route designator denoting an RNAV route. The overlapping symbols for the AMOTT intersection and waypoint indicate that AMOTT can be identified by conventional navigation or by latitude and longitude coordinates. Although coordinates were originally included for aircraft equipped with an inertial navigation system (INS), they are now a good way to cross check between the coordinates on the chart and in the flight management system (FMS) or global positioning system (GPS) databases to ensure you are tracking on your intended en route course. The AMOTT RNAV waypoint includes bearing and distance from the Anchorage VORTAC.
