
## Other Airspeed Limitations

Some important airspeed limitations are not marked on the face of the ASI, but are found on placards and in the AFM/ POH. These airspeeds include:

- Design maneuvering speed \((\mathrm{V}_{\mathrm{A}})\) the maximum speed at which the structural design's limit load can be imposed (either by gusts or full deflection of the control surfaces) without causing structural damage. It is important to consider weight when referencing this speed. For example, \(\mathrm{V}_{\mathrm{A}}\) may be 100 knots when an airplane is heavily loaded, but only 90 knots when the load is light.
