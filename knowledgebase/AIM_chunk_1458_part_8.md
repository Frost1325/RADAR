LOCALIZER- The component of an ILS which provides course guidance to the runway.

(See INSTRUMENT LANDING SYSTEM.)

(See ICAO term LOCALIZER COURSE.)

(Refer to AIM.)

LOCALIZER COURSE [ICAO]- The locus of points, in any given horizontal plane, at which the DDM (difference in depth of modulation) is zero.

LOCALIZER OFFSET- An angular offset of the localizer aligned with \(3^{\circ}\) of the runway alignment.

LOCALIZER TYPE DIRECTIONAL AID- A localizer with an angular offset that exceeds \(3^{\circ}\) . of the runway alignment used for nonprecision instrument approaches with utility and accuracy comparable to a localizer but which are not part of a complete ILS.
