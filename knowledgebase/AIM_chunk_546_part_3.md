3. ADS-B systems integrated with the transponder will automatically set the applicable emergency status when 7500, 7600, or 7700 are entered into the transponder. ADS B systems not integrated with the transponder, or systems with optional emergency codes, will require that the appropriate emergency code is entered through a pilot interface. ADS-B is intended for in-flight and airport surface use. ADS-B systems should be turned "on" - - and remain "on" - - whenever operating in the air and moving on the airport surface. Civil and military Mode A/C transponders and ADS-B systems should be adjusted to the "on" or normal operating position as soon as practical, unless the change to "standby" has been accomplished previously at the request of ATC.
