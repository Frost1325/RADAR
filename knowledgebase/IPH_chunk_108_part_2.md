From this analysis, the computations resulted in a total system accuracy of \(\pm 4.5^{\circ}\) 95 percent of the time and \(\pm 6.7^{\circ}\) 99 percent of the time. The \(4.5^{\circ}\) value became the basis for primary area obstacle clearance criteria, airway and route widths, and the ATC separation procedures. The \(6.7^{\circ}\) value provides secondary obstacle clearance area dimensions.
