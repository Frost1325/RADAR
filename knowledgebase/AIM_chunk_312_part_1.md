## NOTE-

Pilots are advised that \(122.6\mathrm{MHz}\) is a remote receiver located at the Hampton VORTAC site and designed to provide radio coverage between Hampton and Block Island. Flights proceeding beyond Block Island may contact the Bridgeport FSS Radio Sector by transmitting on \(122.1\mathrm{MHz}\) and listening on Groton VOR frequency \(110.85\mathrm{MHz}\) .
