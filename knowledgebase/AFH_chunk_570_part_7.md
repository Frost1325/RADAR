\(\mathbf{V}_{\mathrm{LOF}}\) . Lift- off speed. The speed at which the aircraft departs the runway during takeoff.

\(\mathbf{V}_{\mathrm{MC}}\) . Minimum control airspeed. This is the minimum flight speed at which a twin- engine airplane can be satisfactorily controlled when an engine suddenly becomes inoperative and the remaining engine is at takeoff power.

\(\mathbf{V}_{\mathrm{MD}}\) . Minimum drag speed.

\(\mathbf{V}_{\mathrm{MO}}\) . Maximum operating speed expressed in knots.

\(\mathbf{V}_{\mathrm{NE}}\) . Never- exceed speed. Operating above this speed is prohibited since it may result in damage or structural failure. The red line on the airspeed indicator.

\(\mathbf{V}_{\mathrm{NO}}\) . Maximum structural cruising speed. Do not exceed this speed except in smooth air. The upper limit of the green arc.
