6. Visibility. Prevailing visibility is reported in statute miles with "SM" appended to it.

EXAMPLE- 7SM- seven statute miles 15SM- fifteen statute miles \(1 / 2\mathrm{SM} - \mathrm{one - half}\) statute mile
