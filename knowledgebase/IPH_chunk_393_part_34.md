Positive Course Guidance (PCG). The continuous display of accurate course and flight path data that enables a pilot to precisely fly along a specific track or course using RNAV, a ground- based NAVAID or a radar vector. For RNAV or a ground- based NAVAID, the guidance is displayed in the aircraft; for a radar vector, the guidance data is displayed in the radar facility

Precision Runway Monitor (PRM). Provides air traffic controllers with high precision secondary surveillance data for aircraft on final approach to parallel runways that have extended centerlines separated by less than 4,300 feet.
