Additionally, the pilot is most likely under considerable self- induced psychological pressure to get the airplane on the ground. The pilot should take this into account and, if possible, allow time to become acclimatized and geographically oriented before attempting an approach and landing, even if it means flying straight and level for a time or circling the airport.
