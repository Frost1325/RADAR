
 Turboprop and turboshaft engines are designed to produce torque for driving a propeller. Torquemeters are calibrated in percentage units, foot- pounds, or psi.

## \(N_{1}\) Indicator

\(N_{1}\) represents the rotational speed of the low pressure compressor and is presented on the indicator as a percentage of design rpm. After start, the speed of the low pressure compressor is governed by the \(\mathrm{N_1}\) turbine wheel. The \(\mathrm{N_1}\) turbine wheel is connected to the low pressure compressor through a concentric shaft.

## \(N_{2}\) Indicator

\(N_{2}\) represents the rotational speed of the high pressure compressor and is presented on the indicator as a percentage of design rpm. The high pressure compressor is governed by the \(\mathrm{N_2}\) turbine wheel. The \(\mathrm{N_2}\) turbine wheel is connected to the high pressure compressor through a concentric shaft.
