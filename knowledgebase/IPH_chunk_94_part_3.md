Keeping track of altitude and position when climbing, and during all other phases of flight, are basic elements of situational awareness (SA). Aircraft equipped with an enhanced ground proximity warning system (EGPWS), terrain awareness and warning system (TAWS), or traffic alert and collision avoidance system (TCAS) help pilots detect and/or correct for potential unsafe proximities to other aircraft and increases pilot(s) situational awareness. Regardless of equipment, pilots must always maintain SA regarding their location and the location of traffic in their vicinity.
