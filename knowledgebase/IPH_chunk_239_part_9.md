ICAO code, Airport Name, Temperature Restriction in Celsius/Fahrenheit and affected Segment. One temperature may apply to multiple segments. Italicized airports have two affected segments, each with a different temperature restrictions. The warmest temperature will be indicated on Airport IAPs next to a snowflake symbol, \(\Theta 3 - 35^{\circ}C\) in the United States Terminal Procedure Publication. The ICON will be added to the TPPs incrementally each charting cycle.
