HIWAS does not replace preflight or inflight weather briefings from FSS or real- time weather updates from Enroute Flight Advisory Service (EFAS), radio call "Flight Watch." Pilots should call FSS or Flight Watch if there are any questions about weather that is different than forecasted or if the HIWAS broadcast appears to be in error.
