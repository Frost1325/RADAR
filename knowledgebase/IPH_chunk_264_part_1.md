## Required Obstacle Clearance (ROC)

The ROC is 1,000 feet for the primary initial segment. The secondary area ROC starts at the primary ROC surface tapering to zero at the edges of the secondary area in both initial and intermediate areas. In the primary area of the intermediate segment, the ROC is 500 feet.
