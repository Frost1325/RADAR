# (w) RIF/ The route details to the revised destination aerodrome, followed by the ICAO four-letter location indicator of the aerodrome. The revised route is subject to reclearance in flight.

EXAMPLE- 1. RIF/DTA HEC KLAX 2. RIF/ESP G94 CLA YPPH

(x) RMK/ Any other plain-language remarks when required by the ATC or deemed necessary.

EXAMPLE-

1. RMK/NRP

2. RMK/DRVSN

(y) RVR/ The minimum RVR requirement of the flight in meters. This item is defined by Eurocontrol, not ICAO. The FAA does not require or use this item, but will accept it in a flight plan.
