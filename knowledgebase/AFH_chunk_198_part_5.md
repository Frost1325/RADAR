Before performing any maneuver, the pilot should complete the required checklist items, make any radio announcements (such as on a practice area frequency), and safety clearing turns. As a general note, a ground reference maneuver should not exceed a bank angle of \(45^{\circ}\) or an airspeed greater than the maneuvering speed. As part of preflight planning, the pilot should determine the predicted (POH/AFM) stall speed at \(50^{\circ}\) or at the highest bank angle expected during the maneuver to assure there will be a safety margin above the stall speed during the maneuver.
