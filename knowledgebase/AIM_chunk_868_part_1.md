## EXAMPLE-

1. Controllers will not clear aircraft direct to THIRD because that waypoint begins the RF leg, and aircraft cannot be vectored or cleared to TURN or vectored to intercept the approach segment at any point between THIRD and FORTH because this is the RF leg.

2. Controllers can clear Aircraft 1 direct to SCOND because the distance to THIRD, where the RF leg begins is 3NM or greater and the intercept angle will be 30 degrees or less and is radar monitored. Controllers can clear Aircraft 2 direct to FIRST because the intercept angle is 90 degrees or less, and the distance from FIRST to THIRD is 6NM or greater.
