 G- limiter may also be incorporated into the system to prevent the pitch down generated by the stick pusher from imposing excessive loads on the aircraft. A "stick shaker," on the other hand, provides stall warning when the airspeed is five to seven percent above stall speed.

## Mach Buffet Boundaries

Mach buffet is a function of the speed of the airflow over the wing—not necessarily the speed of the aircraft. Any time that too great a lift demand is made on the wing, whether from too fast an airspeed or from too high an AOA near the \(\mathrm{M_{MO}}\) , the "high- speed" buffet occurs. There are also occasions when the buffet can be experienced at much lower speeds known as the "low- speed Mach buffet."
