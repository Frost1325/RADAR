This definition still applies to airplanes certified under that regulation. There is no requirement under either determination that the airplane be capable of climbing at this airspeed. \(\mathrm{V}_{\mathrm{MC}}\) only addresses directional control. Further discussion of \(\mathrm{V}_{\mathrm{MC}}\) as determined during airplane certification and demonstrated in pilot training follows later in this chapter.
